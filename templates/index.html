Welcome to OreAtlas!

Explore global mineral production data with interactive maps.

OreAtlas was made as a final project for CS50's Introduction to Computer Science, an online course offered by Harvard University. The source data is accessible on www.world-mining-data.info and in the annual publication "World Mining Data" by the Austrian Federal Ministry of Finance. Please consult their website for additional information. OreAtlas displays mining data for the year 2022.
